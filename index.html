About The Company

Sreedharan & Company Pvt. Limited was promoted by Mr. K. Sreedharan, a leading businessman in North Kerala having diversified business activities, such as Liquor trading, Coconut Oils mill, Textiles etc. and Hotels, Cinema Theaters for more than five decades. As part of his desire to expand the business further, he decided to set up his own manufacturing facilities for Indian Made Foreign Liquor.
